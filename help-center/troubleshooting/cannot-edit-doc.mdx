---
title: Cannot Edit Document
description: Fix document editing access issues.
---

## Symptoms

- "Read-only" error when editing
your document
- Edit buttons are disabled

## Possible Causes

- Not signed in
- Insufficient permissions
- Document locked by another user

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check sign-in status">
    ````
    Make sure you are logged in to your Michael Chen account.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review permissions">
    ````
    Ask the owner to grant you editing access.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait if locked">
    ````
    If someone else is editing, try again in a few minutes.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
