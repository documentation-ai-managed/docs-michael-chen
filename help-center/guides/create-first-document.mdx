## Quickstart Guide

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Dashboard">
    ````
    Log in and open your dashboard at https://michaelchen.com/dashboard
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'New Document'">
    ````
    Press the 'New Document' button at the top right.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter details and save">
    ````
    Fill in the title and content. Click 'Save'.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
