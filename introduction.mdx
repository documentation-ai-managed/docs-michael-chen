## Overview

Michael Chen provides a centralized space to organize, manage, and collaborate on all your project documentation. You create dedicated spaces for different projects, write markdown-powered pages, and keep everything version-controlled and searchable.

Teams use Michael Chen to streamline knowledge sharing, reduce onboarding time, and maintain living documentation that evolves with your projects. Access your docs via a clean web interface at `https://docs.example.com`.

## Navigation and Basic Concepts

Understand core navigation quickly to start using Michael Chen effectively.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Spaces" icon="folder">
    Spaces act as top-level containers. Navigate via the sidebar to switch between projects.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Pages" icon="file-text">
    Pages are markdown documents. Use the page tree for hierarchy and quick jumps.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Search" icon="search">
    Press `<kbd>Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>K</kbd>` or click the magnifying glass to search instantly.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Pin frequently used spaces to the top of your sidebar for faster access.
</Callout>

## Quick Start Preview

Get up and running in minutes. Follow these steps to create your first space.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Visit `https://dashboard.example.com` and create an account with your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a Space" icon="folder-plus">
    Click "New Space" in the sidebar. Name it after your project, like "My Project Docs".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Your First Page" icon="file-plus">
    Inside the space, create a new page. Use markdown to add content.

    <CodeGroup tabs="Markdown,HTML">
      ```markdown
      # Welcome to My Project

      This is your first documentation page.

      ## Quick Links
      - [API Reference](/api)
      - [Changelog](/changelog)
      ```
      ```html
      <h1>Welcome to My Project</h1>
      <p>This is your first documentation page.</p>
      <h2>Quick Links</h2>
      <ul>
        <li><a href="/api">API Reference</a></li>
        <li><a href="/changelog">Changelog</a></li>
      </ul>
      ```
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish and Share" icon="share-2">
    Click "Publish" to make your space live. Share the URL with your team.
  </Step>
</Steps>
