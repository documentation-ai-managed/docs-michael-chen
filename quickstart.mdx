## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
Ensure you have:
- A valid email address for registration
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- Internet connection for account setup and dashboard access
</Callout>

Michael Chen makes it easy to create organized documentation spaces. Follow these steps to register, log in, and build your first project.

## Step 1: Register Your Account

Sign up quickly using your email.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Registration Page" icon="user-plus">
    Navigate to the Michael Chen sign-up page at `https://app.michaelchen.com/register`.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="edit-3">
    Provide your email, create a strong password, and complete any CAPTCHA verification.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification email and click the link to activate your account.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
Save your credentials securely. Use a password manager for best security.
</Callout>

## Step 2: Log In and Explore the Dashboard

Access your new account and familiarize yourself with the interface.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Go to `https://app.michaelchen.com/login` and enter your email and password.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Dashboard Tour" icon="layout">
    You'll see the main dashboard with sections for Projects, Spaces, and Settings. Key areas include:
    
    - **Projects**: Create and manage documentation projects
    - **Spaces**: Organize content into structured documentation sites
    - **Search**: Quickly find pages and files
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Step 3: Create Your First Documentation Project

Start building your docs.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="New Project" icon="plus">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Click New Project" icon="file-plus">
        From the dashboard, select **New Project**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Name and Configure">
        Enter a project name like `My First Docs`, select a template (e.g., Starter Kit), and choose your brand color `#438EEA`.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Generate Pages">
        Click **Create** to auto-generate sample pages like introduction and quickstart.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Import Existing" icon="upload">
    Upload Markdown files or connect to GitHub for seamless import.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Step 4: Edit and Save Your First Page

Customize content using the built-in editor.

<CodeGroup tabs="Markdown Editor,Visual Editor">
  ````markdown
  # Welcome Page

  ## Overview

  This is your customizable documentation.

  - Feature 1
  - Feature 2
  ````

  ````html
  <h2>Visual Editing</h2>
  <p>Drag and drop components, format text, and preview changes live.</p>
  ````
</CodeGroup>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Editor" icon="edit">
    Navigate to your project, select a page (e.g., `/introduction.mdx`), and click **Edit**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Make Changes" icon="save">
    Add headings, lists, or code blocks. Preview in real-time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish" icon="upload-cloud">
    Click **Save and Publish** to make changes live.
  </Step>
</Steps>
